Bio

Travis Butler is an audio engineer, producer, and composer from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He is a graduate from Duquesne University with a Bachelor degree of Music in Sound Recording Technology.

Travis began his musical endeavours with drum lessons in 5th grade. In high school, he began experimenting with digital audio workstations and took a passion in recording music. At Duquesne, he studied audio for 4 years from a diverse set of qualified professors.

Travis is excited to begin working with anyone interested in collaborating. He offers a wide variety of services including recording, mixing, and post-production, and is now also offering beat purchases. Contact Travis now for any audio services you may be interested in!
